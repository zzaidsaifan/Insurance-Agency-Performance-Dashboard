## Overview

The Insurance Agency Performance Dashboard is a business intelligence and financial reporting project designed to analyze insurance agency performance through interactive dashboards and KPI-driven reporting.

The project evaluates key insurance metrics including premium generation, claims performance, customer retention, growth trends, and vendor contributions. The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by transforming raw insurance data into actionable business insights.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze insurance premium performance across multiple product lines.
- Measure underwriting profitability using loss ratio analysis.
- Evaluate customer retention trends.
- Monitor agency growth and business expansion.
- Assess vendor performance and contribution.
- Build executive-level dashboards for performance reporting.

---

## Dashboard Components

### 1. Executive Dashboard

Provides a consolidated view of key business metrics including:

- Total Premium Written
- Total Claims Incurred
- Loss Ratio
- Customer Retention Rate
- Growth Rate
- Vendor Performance Indicators

---

### 2. Premium Analysis

Analyzes premium generation across:

- Product Lines
- States
- Insurance Categories

Key Questions Answered:

- Which products generate the highest premiums?
- Which states contribute the most revenue?
- What is the premium distribution across product categories?

---

### 3. Loss Ratio Analysis

Evaluates underwriting profitability using:

**Loss Ratio Formula**

Loss Ratio = Claims Incurred / Premium Earned

Insights Generated:

- Product profitability comparison
- High-risk insurance segments
- Claims impact analysis

---

### 4. Retention Analysis

Measures customer loyalty and policy renewal behavior.

Metrics Included:

- Retention Rate
- Renewal Trends
- State-wise Retention Performance

---

### 5. Vendor Analysis

Assesses business partner performance by evaluating:

- Vendor Contributions
- Service Performance
- Business Volume Distribution

---

### 6. Growth Trend Analysis

Tracks agency performance over time.

Metrics Included:

- Premium Growth
- Revenue Trends
- Business Expansion Indicators

---

### 7. KPI Summary

Provides a snapshot of key performance indicators for executive reporting.

---

## Key Performance Indicators

| KPI | Description |
|------|------------|
| Total Premium | Total premium generated by the agency |
| Claims Incurred | Total claims paid |
| Loss Ratio | Claims divided by premium |
| Retention Rate | Percentage of customers retained |
| Growth Rate | Change in premium over time |
| Vendor Contribution | Vendor business contribution |
